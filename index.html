Mr. Mukesh Singh

Founder & Fitness Coach — DEO GYM

Mr. Mukesh Singh is a visionary fitness coach with over a decade of experience transforming bodies, minds, and lives. Born and raised in Gorakhpur, he witnessed the lack of premium, result-driven fitness facilities and resolved to change that — founding DEO GYM in 2020 with one bold mission: to make world-class fitness accessible to every individual in the region.

A certified personal trainer and nutrition specialist, Mr. Singh's coaching philosophy is rooted in discipline, consistency, and scientific programming. He has personally guided hundreds of individuals — from beginners to competitive athletes — through transformative fitness journeys. His approach blends traditional strength fundamentals with modern training methodologies, ensuring that every member at DEO GYM gets not just a workout, but a lifestyle upgrade.

WHAT WE OFFER

Our Premium Services

🏃

Adult Sports

Unleash your competitive spirit with our structured adult sports program. Designed to improve agility, endurance, and team coordination, our sports training integrates fitness with the thrill of athletic performance. Perfect for those who want fitness to feel like play.

🤸

Aerobics

High-energy aerobics sessions designed to torch calories, improve cardiovascular health, and boost your mood. Guided by certified instructors, our aerobics classes blend rhythm, movement, and science to deliver maximum fat-burning results in every session.

🚴

Cycling

Power through intense indoor cycling sessions that target your lower body, elevate your heart rate, and build incredible stamina. Our cycling program is suitable for all fitness levels and offers a low-impact, high-reward workout for legs, core, and cardiovascular system.

💃

Dance Fitness

Experience the joy of movement with our dance fitness classes. Combining popular dance styles with structured fitness routines, this program helps you burn calories while having the time of your life. Ideal for those who believe fitness should be fun and expressive.

🏋️

Personal Training

Work one-on-one with our certified trainers to achieve your goals faster. Each personal training session is custom-designed around your body type, fitness level, and objectives — ensuring maximum efficiency, safety, and consistent progress every single session.

🎯

Private Lessons

Our exclusive private lesson program offers focused, distraction-free training for individuals who prefer a private, boutique fitness experience. Ideal for beginners learning fundamentals or advanced athletes refining technique under elite supervision.

💪

Weight Training

Build the physique you've always dreamed of with our scientifically programmed weight training regimen. Whether your goal is hypertrophy, strength, or toning, our trainers design progressive overload programs that ensure your muscles are always challenged and growing.

🤼

Arm Wrestling

Sharpen your grip strength, forearm power, and competitive edge with our dedicated arm wrestling training. Guided by experienced coaches, this unique program combines specialized exercises, technique drills, and competitive sparring to develop true upper-body dominance.
